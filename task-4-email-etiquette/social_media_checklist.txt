Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

Do’s:
- Use a professional profile picture
- Keep your profile information accurate
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others while commenting
- Maintain privacy settings
- Think before posting anything online

Don’ts:
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Avoid using abusive language
- Do not click on suspicious links
- Do not overshare private details
