Cybercrime Prevention Tips

- Always verify email sender before clicking any link
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTP, passwords, or personal details
- Keep your software and apps updated
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
- Install antivirus and security software
- Regularly check your bank statements and accounts
- Report any suspicious activity immediately

Helpline:
- National Cybercrime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
